BASIC FIND
---------------------------
Your query should return only the fields FirstName, LastName, and Age from your collection for all users who are above the
age of 25 ordered by Age in ascending order. Your query will be excuted via the runCommand function.

FIND AND MODIFY
-----------------------------
Your query should return perform a findAndModify operation on your collection. Find the document where the Age = 30 and then
add a field called canRent and set its value to true. Your query will be excuted via the runCommand function.
